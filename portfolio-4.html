Northwestern Intervarsity Keychains

  • Christine Hickernell

  • December 2017

Students studied for finals before winter break, I wanted to create a gift to destress. Made 1/8" wood, these keychains were laser cut with the Northwestern and Intervarsity logos.

Carvey Name Plates

  • Christine Hickernell

  • September 2017

For gifts, I learned how to design name plates. These maple blocks were CNC-ed with Carvey, sanded and coated with a veneer finish.

Hexagon Coasters

  • Christine Hickernell & Joshua Kim

  • March 12, 2017

As the exec board of our Intervarsity chapter finished up their term, we wanted to show our appreciation with a gift. Made from African mahogany, these coasters' hexagonal shape allow for tessellation and various trivets depending on the plate.
