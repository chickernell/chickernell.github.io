Industrial Design Sketches

Outdoor Furniture for the Adventurer

  • Christine Hickernell

  • November 2018

This furniture was designed for the camper who loves adventure but lives away from nature. When upright, the table's legs look like the tent's frame with the top being a mini fireplace. To accomodate more seating space, the table can be pushed down into a bench with the chairs collapsing into the same height. Other designs iterations are attached.

DASH Dog House Design Sprint

  • Christine Hickernell

  • Novemember 2018

This design sprint was designed for DASH the Dog. The top is a tennis ball thrower to allow for independent play time. The wooden staircase emulates Hermes' winged shoes to symbolize speed.

Bluetooth Speaker for My 13 year-old Self Design Sprint

  • Christine Hickernell & Joshua Kim

  • March 12, 2017

This design sprint was designed for my middle school self. When I was around 13, I was a pack rat and loved to collect coins and little trinkets. This bluetooth speaker uses rubber inside to store the trinkets and dampen speaker vibration.
